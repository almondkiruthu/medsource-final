Our Members Journey.

  • WHY BECOME A MEDSOURCE MEMBER?

    MedSource offers a comprehensive and competitive portfolio of pharmaceutical products negotiated with manufacturers and distributors. In addition to leveraging combined purchasing power based on the aggregated volume of its membership, MedSource works with with its partner suppliers to provide:

    • Access to quality products for its members – MedSource offers a catalogue of quality products that its members can order from prequalified distributors and manufacturers
    • Training on business skills, pharmacy practices, and inventory management – MedSource, in partnership with manufacturers, distributors, and financial institutions, offers various professional trainings as well as market intelligence for informed business decisions
    • Access to credit via its financial partners
    • Information technology solutions (data collection at the outlet and corporate office levels) and improved integration and traceability of transactions

  • WHO CAN BECOME A MEDSOURCE MEMBER?

    Membership is open to:

    • Pharmacies
    • Community Pharamcies
    • Pharamacy chains
    • Hospitals and hospital groups
    • Insitutional/in-house clinics
    • Dispensaries
    • Clinical laboratories
    • Clinics
    • Franchise clinics
    • Networks and chains.

    Prospective members must be fully licensed by the Pharmacy and Poisons Board and other relevant regulatory authorities.

  • WHAT TO EXPECT AS MEDSOURCE MEMBER.

    • Access to a streamlined catalogue of pharmaceutical products available from MedSource’s suppliers at favorable prices
    • Access to credit from MedSource-approved suppliers
    • An enterprise resource planning system that assists members in ordering supplies and manage stock and financial records
    • A simplified procurement process for MedSource catalog products
    • Assurance of quality pharmaceutical supplies
    • Up-to-date information and market research on the latest trends and products in the pharmaceutical industry.

  • HOW TO BECOME A MEDSOURCE MEMBER

    Prospective members are invited to complete the online application form. A representative from the MedSource membership team will contact the applicant to complete the process.

SUPPLIERS.

  • WHY BECOME A MEDSOURCE DISTRIBUTOR?

    • Pharmacies, clinics, and hospitals need to efficiently control costs while delivering safe and effective patient care. Distributors are instrumental in meeting these needs.
    • MedSource works to add value across the entire supply chain by maintaining strong and mutually beneficial relationships. MedSource welcomes suppliers of pharmaceutical products and medical/surgical supplies to partner with us to help enhance marketplace competition and offer affordable and quality pharmaceutical solutions to our members.

  • Why Become a MedSource Approved Manufacturer?

    • Improved volumes through consolidated activity by MedSource members.
    • Additional channel for sharing product information with customers/patients in coordination with retailers and distributors
    • MedSource will only list a very limited number of products for each type of medicine and pharmaceutical product in its catalogue to ensure that product volume is delivered to its contracted manufacturers while providing quality, favorably priced product access to end users.

  • BENEFITS OF BECOMING A MEDSOURCE DISTRIBUTOR.

    • Access to quality retailers
    • Improved sales volumes for competitively quoted products through MedSource members
    • Risk management for the distributor – MedSource facilitates provision of credit for its members through bank guarantees and other financial instruments
    • Data analytics – MedSource, through its enterprise resource planning system, provides previously unavailable market intelligence that enables its members make informed business decisions
    • Improved forecasting by aggregation of fragmented demand
